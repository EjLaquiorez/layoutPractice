The Framework

The 7-Step Landing Page Blueprint

Follow this exact sequence to build pages that convert.

1

Define Your Single Goal

Before you write a single word, decide on one action you want visitors to take. One page, one goal.

Pro Tip: Write your goal on a sticky note and keep it visible while building.
2

Know Your Visitor's Pain

Map out exactly who is visiting and what problem brought them here.

Pro Tip: Use the exact language your customers use in support tickets and reviews.
3

Craft a Magnetic Headline

Your headline should promise a specific outcome or transformation your visitor wants.

Pro Tip: Test your headline by asking "Would my ideal customer stop scrolling for this?"
4

Design a Clear Visual Hierarchy

Guide the eye from headline to CTA with size, color, and spacing that creates a natural reading flow.

Pro Tip: Squint at your page—if the CTA doesn't stand out, your hierarchy is broken.
5

Write Benefit-Focused Copy

Focus on what the visitor gains, not what you do. Lead with outcomes, not features.

Pro Tip: Use the "So what?" test on every line—if you can't answer it, cut it.
6

Add Social Proof

Include testimonials, case studies, or trust badges that reduce perceived risk and build credibility.

Pro Tip: Specific numbers beat vague praise—"Increased revenue by 47%" wins over "Great service!"
7

Make the CTA Impossible to Miss

Your call-to-action should be the obvious next step with zero confusion about what happens next.

Pro Tip: Use action verbs + value—"Get My Free Blueprint" beats "Submit" every time.
